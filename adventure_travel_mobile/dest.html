Destinations

Europe
Walk across Icelandic glaciers, ski along the Alps, or climb the High Tatras of Slovakia. With the wide forests of the east, the frozen tundra of the north, and the Mediterranean coast in the south, you can experience any adventure in Europe.

Asia
Picture the frozen wastes of Siberia, the daunting peaks of the Himalayas, the breathtaking waterways of Indonesia, and the limestone cliffs of Thailand. The natural wonders of Asia await you.

Africa
A world of mighty beasts and untamed landscapes can be found in Africa. Try shark-cage diving in South Africa, safari the Serengeti in Tanzania, trek through Uganda to see the gorillas. Make your own discoveries in this last frontier of adventure.

Australia
Australia is known for its adrenaline junkies, but there are adventures out there for everyone. Try horseback riding along the Oodnadatta Track, take a camel across The Red Center, or snorkel along Nigaloo Reef.

North America
Hike the rugged West Coast Trail in Canada, climb the giant sequoias of California, walk the Juneau Icefield of Alaska. Adventure through the overwhelming majesty of the North American Wilderness (but watch out for bears!).

South America
Incredible landscapes await you in South America. Hike the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu, landsail across Pampa del Leoncito, or whitewater raft the Mendoza River. Watch out for the llamas, they spit!
