Spelunking in Thrihnukagigur, Iceland
Interested in descending into a volcano? Have no fear, Iceland's volcano Thrihnukagigur hasn't erupted in 4000 years. Rappel 700 feet into the belly of this beast and observe its geological beauty.

Bogwalking in Lahemaa, Estonia
Walk over multi-coloured moss and lichens in Lahemaa Park, Estonia. Boardwalks allow you to hike through the bogs without making a mess of yourself and the eerie landscape will enthrall you.

Caving in Tolmin Gorge, Slovenia
Venture into this beautiful gorge of azure waters. Hike through tunnels and snake through paths until you finally reach Dante's Grotto. This untamed, 1140 meter labyrinth is not for the faint-hearted.

Windsurfing in Tarifa, Spain
Tarifa, the 'Wind Capital City" of Europe, is the ideal spot for any wind-surfer pros or wannabes. Hit the wind and the waves all day out in the lagoon and then experience the thriving nightlife back on shore.

Hot Air Balloon in Cappadocia, Turkey
Hot air ballooning in Cappadocia is a one-of-a-kind experience. You will feel a sense of wonderment and awe float over surreal landscapes of strange rock formations called faerie chimneys.

Climb High Tatra Mountains of Slovakia
The Tatra Mountains are a rock climber's paradise! Although small compared to other mountain ranges, this natural divide between Poland and Slovakia has rock faces that reach up to 900 meters.

Kayaking The Pont du Gard, France
The Pont du Gard is the perfect stopover on your trip down the meandering Gardon River. The marvelous, three tiered bridge was once a Roman aqueduct and is best viewed on the water.
